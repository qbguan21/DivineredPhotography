BEHIND THE CAMERA

With the clock ticking by, moments come, moments go. Perhaps the only way to seize time is to click on that shutter-release button.We are Divinered. It’s all started since 2009, where we started our journey for wedding, product and portrait photography.
Divinered were created by a group of photographers and graphic designers with great passion for capturing moments and producing great art work. Since founded the company in 2009, it has grown into one of the best photography company in Malaysia. With a group of talented young people, we work together to create beautiful masterpiece. Divinered provides photography service for wedding, portrait, commercial, advertising and products and events. Through this professionalism and expertise, we have grown to become a specialist company in photography and has produced quality photographs in Malaysia and oversea.
